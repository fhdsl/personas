# Clinical Trials Researcher

## Job Title
Clinical Research Associate

## Major Responsibilities
Identifying potential trial sites and participants
Screening and enrolling participants for clinical trials
Collecting and managing study data
Communicating with study participants and investigators
Work, Collaboration and Communication Patterns
Works closely with clinical trial sites, investigators, and study participants. Collaborates with data management and regulatory affairs teams. Communicates frequently via email, phone, and in-person meetings.

## Funding and Reporting Model
Reports to the Director of Clinical Research. Works within a budget provided by upper management.

## Adeptness with using Data and Technology
Comfortable using various electronic data capture (EDC) systems and clinical trial management systems (CTMS). Able to extract and analyze data for screening and enrollment purposes.

## Software Expertise, Attitude and Use
Highly proficient in Microsoft Excel and Word. Has experience with various CTMS and EDC systems. Open to learning new software as needed.

## Approach and Tolerance to Change
Open to change and adaptable to new processes and procedures.

## Broad Needs and Wants
Efficient and effective systems to identify and screen potential trial participants. Accurate and timely data to inform decision-making.

## Broad Challenges
Identifying and screening potential participants who meet study criteria. Collecting and managing study data from multiple sites and sources.

## Metrics of Success and Motivations
Successful enrollment of participants who meet study criteria. Positive feedback from clinical trial sites and investigators.

## Professional Development Goals
Stay up to date with industry trends and regulations. Learn new skills and technologies to improve participant screening and enrollment. Gain experience with innovative approaches to clinical trial design and implementation.
