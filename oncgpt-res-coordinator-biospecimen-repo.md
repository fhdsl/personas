# Biospecimen Repository Research Coordinator

Job Title
Research Coordinator

Major Responsibilities
Managing and coordinating biospecimen collection and storage for multiple principal investigators (PIs)
Annotating biospecimens with de-identified patient data
Collaborating with PIs to ensure regulatory compliance for biospecimen collection and storage
Maintaining accurate and up-to-date biospecimen and patient data records
Work, Collaboration and Communication Patterns
Works closely with multiple PIs, clinical research coordinators, and laboratory technicians. Communicates frequently via email, phone, and in-person meetings.

Funding and Reporting Model
Reports to the Director of Biospecimen Repositories. Works within a budget provided by upper management.

Adeptness with using Data and Technology
Comfortable using various electronic data capture (EDC) systems and laboratory information management systems (LIMS). Able to extract and analyze data for reporting purposes.

Software Expertise, Attitude and Use
Highly proficient in Microsoft Excel and Word. Has experience with various LIMS and EDC systems. Open to learning new software as needed.

Approach and Tolerance to Change
Open to change and adaptable to new processes and procedures.

Broad Needs and Wants
Efficient and effective systems to manage biospecimen collection, annotation, and storage. Accurate and timely data to inform decision-making.

Broad Challenges
Managing and coordinating multiple biospecimen repositories for different PIs. Ensuring regulatory compliance for biospecimen collection and storage. Maintaining accurate and up-to-date biospecimen and patient data records.

Metrics of Success and Motivations
Successful management of biospecimen repositories for multiple PIs. Positive feedback from PIs and clinical research coordinators.

Professional Development Goals
Stay up to date with industry trends and regulations. Learn new skills and technologies to improve biospecimen annotation and management. Gain experience with innovative approaches to biospecimen collection and storage.
