# Translational Oncology Research Administrator

## Job Title
Translational Oncology Research Administrator

Major Responsibilities
Identifying the needs and challenges of translational oncology researchers who are organized into groups by disease type
Facilitating communication and collaboration among the research groups and with external partners
Coordinating administrative tasks related to research funding, grant applications, and regulatory compliance
Providing support for the organization of scientific meetings and symposia
Work, Collaboration and Communication Patterns
Works closely with translational oncology researchers, research coordinators, and external partners. Communicates frequently via email, phone, and in-person meetings.

Funding and Reporting Model
Reports to the Director of Translational Oncology Research. Works within a budget provided by upper management.

Adeptness with using Data and Technology
Comfortable using various electronic data capture (EDC) systems and database management tools. Able to extract and analyze data for reporting purposes.

Software Expertise, Attitude and Use
Highly proficient in Microsoft Excel and Word. Has experience with various EDC systems and database management tools. Open to learning new software as needed.

Approach and Tolerance to Change
Open to change and adaptable to new processes and procedures.

Broad Needs and Wants
Effective communication and collaboration among research groups. Support for administrative tasks related to research funding, grant applications, and regulatory compliance. Efficient organization of scientific meetings and symposia.

Broad Challenges
Navigating complex regulations and ethical considerations related to research funding and regulatory compliance. Coordinating communication and collaboration among diverse research groups. Ensuring effective organization and execution of scientific meetings and symposia.

Metrics of Success and Motivations
Positive feedback from translational oncology researchers and external partners. Successful grant applications and funding. Efficient execution of scientific meetings and symposia.

Professional Development Goals
Stay up to date with industry trends and regulations. Learn new skills and technologies to improve research administration. Gain experience with innovative approaches to translational oncology research.
