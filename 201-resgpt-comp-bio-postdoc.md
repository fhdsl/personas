Persona Title: Computational Biology Postdoctoral Fellow
Job Title
Computational Biology Postdoctoral Fellow

Major Responsibilities
Analyze data generated from wet lab experiments using computational techniques
Develop computational models to explain biological phenomena observed in the lab
Work with wet lab staff to design experiments that generate data amenable to computational analysis
Collaborate with other computational biology postdocs to advance the research program of the lab
Publish research results in peer-reviewed journals
Work, Collaboration and Communication Patterns
Works closely with wet lab staff to generate and analyze data
Collaborates with other computational biology postdocs to advance the research program of the lab
Communicates research findings to colleagues and stakeholders
Funding and Reporting Model
Supports research projects funded by grants and donations
Reports progress and results to principal investigators and senior research staff
Adeptness with using Data and Technology
Highly skilled in using computational techniques to analyze biological data
Familiar with wet lab techniques and procedures
Comfortable using a variety of scientific software and tools
Software Expertise, Attitude and Use
Proficient in using scientific software, such as Python, R, and Matlab
Willing to learn new software and technologies as needed
Uses software to enhance research productivity and efficiency
Approach and Tolerance to Change
Open to new ideas and approaches
Adapts to changes in research direction or methodology
Embraces new technologies and tools
Broad Needs and Wants
Opportunities for professional development and career advancement in the field of computational biology
Access to state-of-the-art research equipment and facilities
Recognition and appreciation for contributions to research projects
Broad Challenges
Managing and analyzing large and complex datasets generated from wet lab experiments
Developing computational models that accurately reflect biological reality
Maintaining work-life balance in a demanding research environment
Metrics of Success and Motivations
Number and quality of research papers published
Development of new computational models and techniques
Contributions to the overall mission of the lab
Professional Development Goals
Developing new computational biology skills and expertise
Mentoring and training junior research staff in computational techniques
Advancing to a faculty or research staff position in a leading research institution.
