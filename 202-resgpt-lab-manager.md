# Computational Biology Lab Manager

## Job Title
Lab Manager

Major Responsibilities
Manage daily operations of a computational biology lab with a wet lab component
Supervise and train lab staff on computational and wet lab techniques and procedures
Oversee laboratory safety and compliance with institutional and government regulations
Manage lab inventory, ordering and maintenance of equipment and supplies
Contribute to ongoing research projects as needed
Work, Collaboration and Communication Patterns
Works closely with principal investigators and senior research staff to plan and execute research projects
Collaborates with other lab managers and support staff to ensure smooth lab operations
Communicates effectively with lab staff, colleagues and stakeholders
Funding and Reporting Model
Supports research projects funded by grants and donations
Reports progress and results to principal investigators and senior research staff
Adeptness with using Data and Technology
Highly skilled in using computational techniques to analyze biological data
Familiar with wet lab techniques and procedures
Comfortable using a variety of scientific software and tools
Software Expertise, Attitude and Use
Proficient in using scientific software, such as Python, R, and Matlab
Willing to learn new software and technologies as needed
Uses software to enhance research productivity and efficiency
Approach and Tolerance to Change
Open to new ideas and approaches
Adapts to changes in research direction or methodology
Embraces new technologies and tools
Broad Needs and Wants
Opportunities for professional development and career advancement in the field of lab management
Access to state-of-the-art research equipment and facilities
Recognition and appreciation for contributions to lab operations and research projects
Broad Challenges
Balancing competing demands of managing a complex research lab and contributing to ongoing research projects
Managing personnel and resources effectively to ensure efficient lab operations
Ensuring compliance with institutional and government regulations while maintaining research productivity
Metrics of Success and Motivations
Successful completion of research projects and effective management of lab operations
Development of new lab management skills and expertise
Contributions to the overall mission of the lab
Professional Development Goals
Developing new lab management skills and expertise
Mentoring and training junior research staff in computational and wet lab techniques
Advancing to a leadership or management position within the research institution.
