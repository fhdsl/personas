# Vice President of Precision Oncology

## Job Title
Vice President of Precision Oncology

Major Responsibilities
Developing and implementing precision oncology strategies and initiatives
Ensuring that patients are given the opportunity to consent to participate in research including clinical trials, data-only research projects, and the use of residual clinical samples for research
Developing and maintaining partnerships with external stakeholders in the precision oncology community
Managing a team of researchers and coordinators to oversee precision oncology projects
Work, Collaboration and Communication Patterns
Works closely with researchers, clinicians, and administrators. Communicates frequently via email, phone, and in-person meetings.

Funding and Reporting Model
Reports to the Chief Executive Officer. Works within a budget provided by upper management.

Adeptness with using Data and Technology
Comfortable using various electronic data capture (EDC) systems and database management tools. Able to extract and analyze data for reporting purposes.

Software Expertise, Attitude and Use
Highly proficient in Microsoft Excel and Word. Has experience with various EDC systems and database management tools. Open to learning new software as needed.

Approach and Tolerance to Change
Open to change and adaptable to new processes and procedures.

Broad Needs and Wants
Effective consent processes for research participation. Access to high-quality data and biospecimens for precision oncology initiatives. Strong partnerships with external stakeholders.

Broad Challenges
Navigating complex regulations and ethical considerations related to patient consent for research participation. Managing a diverse team of researchers and coordinators to oversee precision oncology projects. Developing and maintaining partnerships with external stakeholders.

Metrics of Success and Motivations
Increased patient participation in precision oncology research. Publication of successful research findings. Positive feedback from stakeholders in the precision oncology community.

Professional Development Goals
Stay up to date with industry trends and regulations. Learn new skills and technologies to improve precision oncology initiatives. Gain experience with innovative approaches to oncology research.
