# Translational Researcher in Oncology

## Job Title
Professor

Major Responsibilities
Maintaining a biospecimen repository for cancer research
Generating novel large-scale genomic datasets to understand molecular markers of disease
Analyzing genomic data to identify potential therapeutic targets for cancer treatment
Collaborating with clinicians to design and implement clinical trials based on research findings
Work, Collaboration and Communication Patterns
Works closely with laboratory technicians, bioinformaticians, and clinical oncologists. Communicates frequently via email, phone, and in-person meetings.

Funding and Reporting Model
Reports to the Director of Translational Research. Works within a budget provided by upper management.

Adeptness with using Data and Technology
Highly proficient in genomic data analysis, bioinformatics, and database management. Comfortable using various electronic data capture (EDC) systems and laboratory information management systems (LIMS). Able to extract and analyze data for reporting purposes.

Software Expertise, Attitude and Use
Highly proficient in bioinformatics software such as R, Python, and Perl. Has experience with various LIMS and EDC systems. Open to learning new software as needed.

Approach and Tolerance to Change
Open to change and adaptable to new processes and procedures.

Broad Needs and Wants
Access to high-quality biospecimens and genomic data for analyses. Effective collaboration with laboratory technicians, bioinformaticians, and clinical oncologists. Efficient and accurate genomic data analyses and reporting.

Broad Challenges
Integrating and analyzing complex genomic data from various sources. Developing and implementing genomic models that are relevant to clinical research questions. Collaborating with clinicians to design and implement clinical trials based on research findings.

Metrics of Success and Motivations
Publication of successful research findings. Positive feedback from clinical oncologists. Contribution to the development of new cancer therapies.

Professional Development Goals
Stay up to date with industry trends and regulations. Learn new bioinformatics techniques and programming languages to improve genomic data analysis. Gain experience with innovative approaches to oncology research.
