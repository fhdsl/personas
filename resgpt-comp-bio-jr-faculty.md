# Computational Bio Junior Faculty
## Job Title
Computational Lab Leader and Mentor

Major Responsibilities
Lead and direct a busy computational biology lab
Develop and implement research projects in collaboration with lab members and colleagues
Mentor and supervise junior lab members, including postdocs, graduate students, and research staff
Train and advise lab members on the use of software and tools developed by the lab
Work, Collaboration and Communication Patterns
Works closely with lab members, colleagues, and collaborators to plan and execute research projects
Communicates effectively with lab members, stakeholders, and funders
Encourages a collaborative and supportive lab culture
Funding and Reporting Model
Secures funding from grants, donations, and other sources to support lab operations and research projects
Reports progress and results to funding agencies, collaborators, and stakeholders
Adeptness with using Data and Technology
Highly skilled in using computational techniques to analyze biological data
Proficient in developing and using software and tools for computational analysis
Comfortable using a variety of scientific software and tools
Software Expertise, Attitude and Use
Developed software and tools as a postdoc and continues to use them in research projects
Encourages lab members to use and contribute to the development of lab software and tools
Promotes open-source and reproducible research practices
Approach and Tolerance to Change
Open to new ideas and approaches
Adapts to changes in research direction or methodology
Embraces new technologies and tools
Broad Needs and Wants
Opportunities for professional development and career advancement in the field of computational biology
Access to state-of-the-art research equipment and facilities
Recognition and appreciation for contributions to lab operations and research projects
Broad Challenges
Balancing competing demands of leading a busy lab, mentoring junior lab members, and contributing to ongoing research projects
Managing personnel and resources effectively to ensure efficient lab operations
Ensuring compliance with institutional and government regulations while maintaining research productivity
Metrics of Success and Motivations
Successful completion of research projects and effective leadership of the lab
Development of new software and tools for computational analysis
Success and accomplishments of lab members and their research projects
Professional Development Goals
Developing new lab management skills and expertise
Mentoring and training junior lab members in computational techniques and software development
Advancing to a leadership or management position within the research institution.
